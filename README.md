# ML model for Hedging
To avoiding financial risks comapanies hedge their contart. As a Example Kazakhstani company AAA sell oil to Italian company BBB. To prevent or mitigate price volatility both companies hedge their contracts. They have to use derivatives(Futures, Call Option, Put Option, etc) and thier combinations. 

Main purpose of our model is recomending and prediction of the best combitation for current situation rely on provided data.

- Collecting data
- preparing data
- Chosing strategy
- Buiding, training, testing model for hedging oil price with derivatives(Future, Call Option, Put Option)
